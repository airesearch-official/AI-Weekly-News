## 🧠 Massive Memory & Reasoning: SubQ, Grok 4.3 & Recursive MASS
- **SubQ**: A breakthrough in long-context AI, enabling a staggering **12M context window**. This allows for processing massive codebases, legal archives, or entire video series in a single prompt.
- **Grok 4.3 Beta**: xAI's latest update to Grok, featuring native multimodal reasoning and improved "thinking" modes for complex problem solving.
- **Recursive MASS**: A new architecture for recursive model scaling and memory management.
- 🔗 [SubQ AI Official](https://subq.ai/)
- 🔗 [Recursive MASS Project](https://recursivemas.github.io/)

## 🎬 Creative Freedom & Video: Sulphur 2, UniVidX & C2R
- **Sulphur 2 (Uncensored)**: A new open-weight base model for video generation that removes standard safety filters, allowing for unrestricted creative expression in video workflows.
- **UniVidX**: A unified video generation framework designed for high-fidelity, high-motion consistency.
- **C2R (Coarse to Real)**: A framework for transforming coarse sketches or inputs into realistic video outputs.
- **OmniShotCut**: Advanced AI-driven video editing and shot refinement.
- 🤗 [Sulphur-2-base on Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/SulphurAI/Sulphur-2-base)
- 🔗 [UniVidX Project](https://houyuanchen111.github.io/UniVidX.github.io/)
- 🔗 [C2R Project Page](https://gonzalognogales.github.io/coarse2real/)
- 🔗 [OmniShotCut Project](https://uva-computer-vision-lab.github.io/OmniShotCut_website/)
