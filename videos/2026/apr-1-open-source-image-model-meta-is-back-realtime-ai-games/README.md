## 🔥 Overview
This week marks a major turning point as Meta re-enters the high-end AI race with **Muse Spark**, a natively multimodal powerhouse. ## 🧠 Meta is BACK: Muse Spark (MSL)
- **Muse Spark** is the first model from Meta's new **Superintelligence Labs (MSL)**. It's a ground-up overhaul, designed to be natively multimodal (text, image, audio, video) and highly efficient.
- It features advanced "test-time reasoning" using parallel agents to solve complex problems without massive latency spikes.
- 📖 [Introducing Muse Spark (Meta AI Blog)](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/04/introducing-muse-spark-meta-superintelligence-labs/)

---

## 🖼️ New #1 Image Model: LPM 1.0 & Ernie Image
- **LPM 1.0 (Large Performance Model)**: A new open-source leader in image generation, focusing on extreme prompt adherence and artistic fidelity.
- **Ernie Image (Baidu)**: A new high-end image generator from Baidu's latest foundation model series, optimized for complex visual reasoning.
- 🔗 [LPM 1.0 Project Page](https://large-performance-model.github.io/)
- 📖 [Ernie Image Announcement](https://ernie.baidu.com/blog/posts/ernie-image/)

---

## 🎮 Realtime AI Games & Worlds: In-Spatio World
- **In-Spatio World**: A breakthrough in generating realtime, interactive game environments that maintain consistency across exploration.
- **WayPoint 1.5**: Advanced AI for autonomous navigation and waypoint planning in dynamic 3D worlds.
- 🔗 [In-Spatio World Project](https://inspatio.github.io/inspatio-world/)
- 📖 [WayPoint 1.5 Details](https://over.world/blog/waypoint-1-5)

---

## 🎬 Video & Motion: CoMoVi & Long-Term Motion
- **CoMoVi**: A new framework for collaborative motion-to-video generation, pushing the quality of character movements.
- **Long-Term Motion**: Research focusing on maintaining character and object consistency over much longer video durations.
- **OmniShow**: A unified model for high-fidelity video synthesis and editing.
- 🔗 [CoMoVi Project](https://igl-hkust.github.io/CoMoVi/)
- 🔗 [Long-Term Motion Research](https://compvis.github.io/long-term-motion/)
- 🔗 [OmniShow Project](https://correr-zhou.github.io/OmniShow/)

---

## 🧠 Frontier LLMs: MiniMax M2.7 & Numina
- **MiniMax M2.7**: Now available on Hugging Face, this model focuses on "self-evolution" and high-stakes software engineering tasks.
- **Numina**: A new model specifically tuned for high-level mathematical reasoning and complex problem-solving.
- 🤗 [MiniMax M2.7 on Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/MiniMaxAI/MiniMax-M2.7)
- 🔗 [Numina Project](https://h-embodvis.github.io/NUMINA/)

## 🛠️ Specialized AI Tools
- **Spatial Edit**: Precise AI-driven editing for spatial and 3D-aware content.
- **RotorQuant**: A new approach to model quantization (RotorQuant) for extreme efficiency on local hardware.
- **Moss TTS Nano**: An ultra-compact, open-source TTS model for realtime emotional speech.
- **Vanast**: Advanced research into unified floor plan and spatial generation.
