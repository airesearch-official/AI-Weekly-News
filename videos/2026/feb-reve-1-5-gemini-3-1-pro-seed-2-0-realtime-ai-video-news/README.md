## 🖼️ Reve 1.5 & Gemini 3.1 Pro
- **Reve 1.5** is a new heavy-hitter in the image generation space, focusing on ultra-realistic, high-detail images with insane lighting and textures. It's quickly becoming a favorite for photorealistic content.
- **Gemini 3.1 Pro** from Google DeepMind has taken the benchmark crown, offering superior performance in complex tasks, creative concepting, and long-context reasoning.
- 📖 [Reve 1.5 Announcement](https://blog.reve.com/posts/reve-1.5-is-here/)
- 📖 [Gemini 3.1 Pro Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-1-pro/)

---

## 🎬 Seedance 2 & Seed 2.0
- **Seedance 2 (Open Source)** is a game-changing alternative for AI video generation that allows for full local deployment with a massive performance boost.
- **Seed 2.0** by ByteDance introduces refined video generation capabilities, focusing on motion consistency and high-fidelity output.
- 🔗 [Seed 2.0 Official Page](https://seed.bytedance.com/en/seed2)

---

## 🗣️ KittenTTS & AnchorWave & AudioX
- **KittenTTS** is a ultra-fast, open-source realtime text-to-speech model designed for near-zero delay and natural emotional voices.
- **AnchorWave** and **AudioX** are pushing the boundaries of synchronized audio and speech generation for real-time applications.
- 🔗 [KittenTTS GitHub](https://github.com/KittenML/KittenTTS)
- 🔗 [AnchorWave Project Page](https://zunwang1.github.io/AnchorWeave)
- 🔗 [AudioX Project Page](https://zeyuet.github.io/AudioX/)

---

## 🧠 Qwen 3.5 Medium & Tiny-Aya
- **Qwen 3.5 Medium** is Alibaba's latest open-weight MoE model (397B total, 17B active) that delivers frontier performance across coding and multimodal tasks.
- **Tiny-Aya** from Cohere Research is a highly efficient, small-scale model covering 70+ languages, perfect for local multilingual applications.
- 📖 [Qwen 3.5 Blog](https://qwen.ai/blog?id=qwen3.5)
- 📖 [Cohere Tiny-Aya Research](https://cohere.com/research/aya)

## 🎥 Realtime AI & Video Tools
- **Monarch RT** and **LUVE** enable low-latency live video AI, suitable for livestreamers and VTubers.
- **Multi-Shot Master** by the KlingTeam allows for precise control over multi-shot video generation.
- **Mirage (T2V-14B-MoE)** is a powerful text-to-video model leveraging Mixture of Experts for high-quality motion.
- 🔗 [Monarch RT Project](https://infini-ai-lab.github.io/MonarchRT/)
- 🔗 [LUVE Project Page](https://unicornanrocinu.github.io/LUVE_web/)
- 🔗 [Multi-Shot Master GitHub](https://github.com/KlingTeam/MultiShotMaster)
