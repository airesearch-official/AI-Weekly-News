## 🔥 Overview
This week marks a significant milestone in open-source generative AI, particularly in the video and audio domains. We're seeing the release of LTX 2.3, which sets a new bar for open-source video generation with native audio sync. Meanwhile, OpenAI continues to push the frontier with GPT-5.4, focusing on massive context and reasoning improvements. From AI-driven VFX to emotionally intelligent TTS, the tools available to creators are becoming more sophisticated and integrated.

Here’s a full breakdown with source links and insights 👇

---

## 🎬 LTX 2.3: The New Open-Source Video King
- **LTX 2.3** by Lightricks is a SOTA joint audio-visual foundation model. It generates high-quality video where the audio (voice, music, sound effects) natively defines the structure and pacing.
- It supports up to 4K resolution, native audio, and is built for production-grade workflows rather than just short clips.
- 🔗 [LTX 2.3 Official Page](https://ltx.io/model/ltx-2-3)
- 🤗 [LTX-2 on Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/Lightricks/LTX-2)

---

## 🧠 GPT-5.4 & Reasoning Frontiers
- **GPT-5.4** brings major updates to OpenAI's flagship model, featuring a 1-million-token context window and significant leaps in factual accuracy and agentic capabilities.
- It is designed to handle entire codebases and complex legal/technical libraries in a single session.
- 📖 [Introducing GPT-5.4](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-4/)

---

## 🗣️ Next-Gen Audio: Fish Audio S2 & Tada
- **Fish Audio S2 (Fish Speech)**: A SOTA open-source TTS trained on over 10 million hours of audio across 50+ languages. It uses a Dual-Autoregressive architecture for emotionally rich, natural speech.
- **Tada (Hume AI)**: An open-source release from Hume AI focusing on empathic and expressive voice synthesis.
- 🔗 [Fish Speech GitHub](https://github.com/fishaudio/fish-speech)
- 📖 [Hume AI Tada Announcement](https://www.hume.ai/blog/opensource-tada)

---

## 🎥 AI Video Editing & VFX
- **Free-Edit** and **Kiwi-Edit**: New frameworks for flexible and precise AI-driven video editing.
- **MatAnyone 2**: An improved tool for high-quality video matting and background removal.
- **Diffusion Harmonizer (NVIDIA)**: A research breakthrough for seamlessly blending AI-generated elements into existing footage with consistent lighting and texture.
- 🔗 [Free-Edit Project](https://free-edit.github.io/page/)
- 🔗 [Kiwi-Edit Project](https://showlab.github.io/Kiwi-Edit/)
- 🔗 [NVIDIA Diffusion Harmonizer](https://research.nvidia.com/labs/sil/projects/diffusion-harmonizer/)

---

## 🖼️ Image Editing & 3D Generation
- **FireRed Image Edit 1.1**: A powerful update to the FireRed team's image editing suite, offering better control over localized edits.
- **PixAR Mesh**: A new approach to generating high-quality 3D meshes from images, focusing on geometry and texture accuracy.
- 🤗 [FireRed Image Edit 1.1](https://huggingface.co/FireRedTeam/FireRed-Image-Edit-1.1)
- 🔗 [PixAR Mesh Project](https://mlpc-ucsd.github.io/PixARMesh/)
