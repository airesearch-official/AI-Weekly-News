## 🔥 Overview
This week in AI feels like a leap into a sci-fi future. We're seeing the emergence of true "World Models" that can simulate reality with interactive consistency, alongside massive efficiency gains in frontier language models. From OpenAI's new "Mini" powerhouses to AI that can navigate Google Maps like a human, the theme of this week is **Interactive Real-Time Intelligence**.

## 🧠 GPT-5.4 Mini & Nano
- **GPT-5.4 Mini** is the new mid-tier efficiency king from OpenAI, bringing near-Opus level reasoning to a much faster and cheaper package.
- **GPT-5.4 Nano** is optimized for extreme on-device performance, enabling high-quality reasoning on mobile hardware.
- 📖 [Introducing GPT-5.4](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-4/)

---

## 🌍 World Models & "GTA in Real Life"
- **Soul World Model (SWM)** and **In-Spatio World FM** are pushing the boundaries of consistent environmental simulation, allowing for "playable" real-world scenarios generated by AI.
- **Genie 3 (Google DeepMind)**: A general-purpose world model that generates interactive environments at 24 FPS from text prompts.
- 🔗 [Soul World Model Project](https://seoul-world-model.github.io/)
- 🔗 [In-Spatio World FM](https://inspatio.github.io/worldfm/)
- 📖 [Genie 3 Announcement](https://deepmind.google/blog/genie-3-a-new-frontier-for-world-models/)

---

## 🤖 Agentic AI: PageAgent & Ask Maps
- **PageAgent (Alibaba)**: A new agentic framework that can interact with and complete tasks across web pages and complex UI environments.
- **Ask Maps (Google)**: An immersive navigation AI that allows users to interact with Google Maps using natural language to explore and navigate.
- 🔗 [PageAgent GitHub](https://alibaba.github.io/page-agent/)
- 📖 [Google Ask Maps Blog](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/maps/ask-maps-immersive-navigation/)

---

## 🎬 Real-Time Video & Motion
- **FlashMotion** and **ShotVerse**: New tools for ultra-fast, multi-shot AI video generation with improved character and motion consistency.
- **Wild-Actor**: High-fidelity human motion generation for realistic digital actors.
- 🔗 [FlashMotion Project](https://quanhaol.github.io/flashmotion-site/)
- 🔗 [ShotVerse Project](https://shotverse.github.io/)
- 🔗 [Wild-Actor Project](https://wildactor.github.io/)

## 🛠️ Efficient Local AI
- **Mistral Small 4**: A new 119B parameter model that matches or surpasses GPT-OSS 120B while being significantly more efficient in its outputs.
- **Nemotron-3 Super (NVIDIA)**: A hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE model designed for advanced agentic reasoning.
- 📖 [Mistral Small 4 Release](https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-small-4)
- 📖 [Introducing Nemotron-3 Super](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/introducing-nemotron-3-super-an-open-hybrid-mamba-transformer-moe-for-agentic-reasoning/)

## 🚀 Wrap Up
This week shows that we are moving from "static generation" to "interactive simulation."
- **Efficiency is the new scale**: GPT-5.4 Mini and Mistral Small 4 prove that performance can come in smaller, faster packages.
- **Playable Reality**: World models like Genie 3 and SWM are turning generative AI into a tool for building entire interactive realities.
- **Agents are here**: Between PageAgent and Ask Maps, AI is finally starting to navigate our digital and physical worlds for us.
