## 🎨 Creative & Design Breakthroughs
- **VecGlypher**: A single multimodal model that generates editable SVG glyph outlines directly from text prompts—no rasterization needed.
- **Doc-to-LoRA**: A revolutionary method from Sakana AI to instantly update LLMs with new knowledge by converting documents into LoRA weights.
- **DreamID-Omni**: A unified framework for controllable human-centric audio-video generation.
- 🔗 [VecGlypher Project](https://xk-huang.github.io/VecGlypher/)
- 🔗 [Doc-to-LoRA](https://pub.sakana.ai/doc-to-lora/)
- 🔗 [DreamID-Omni Project](https://guoxu1233.github.io/DreamID-Omni/)
